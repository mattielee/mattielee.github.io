Cornell Custom Silicon Systems (C2S2)

Digital Subteam Member

  • SystemVerilog
  • ASIC Design Flow
  • Register Retiming
  • Open-source tools
  • Python

C2S2 is a student-run project team that uses open-source tools to tape out custom silicon chips. C2S2 is a unique opportunity for me to learn about ASIC Design outside of the classroom. C2S2 is much less structured than a regular college course, which means I am able to learn much more about the tools that we use because I am doing everything from scratch. I also appreciate the collaborative learning culture of C2S2. Everyone is willing to teach each other what they know and explain what they are working on, even across different disciplines. Learn more about my C2S2 projects on my projects page.
